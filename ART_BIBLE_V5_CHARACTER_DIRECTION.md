# Art Bible V5 — Contemporary Faceted Characters

## Source of truth

`assets/art_bible/character_options/character-directions-v1.png` is the canonical character sheet for every visible person in the garden. This document overrides the hooded-robed character direction in V4 while retaining V4's rules for restrained acting, factual reflection and environmental response.

## Silhouette

- Youthful human proportions, approximately 7–8 heads tall.
- Clearly separated head, torso, arms, trousers and grounded footwear.
- Relaxed contemporary clothing: cropped jackets, loose shirts, knit vests, layered paper-like scarves and tapered wide trousers.
- Every character has a distinct hair silhouette and outfit construction.
- Hands remain anatomically restrained and appear only when the pose needs them.

## Material and color

- Matte watercolor surfaces with visible paper grain and gently faceted planes.
- Warm ivory is the dominant clothing value.
- Sage, mist blue and mineral teal form the secondary palette.
- Coral appears only as a small seam, belt, scarf or lining accent.
- Shoes use quiet ivory, sage or blue-grey materials with visible soles.

## Emotional states

- Closed states use lowered gaze, gathered shoulders and hands held near the face or torso.
- Open states lift the gaze and extend one believable hand.
- Do not use tears, bouncing heads, emoji faces or theatrical gestures.
- Preserve the same identity and clothing before and after interaction.

## Runtime requirement

The wallet character, Quiet Shelter character, the smaller person in The Circle and all four surrounding people must use this same family. The characters remain procedural real-time 3D models; the reference sheet is never displayed as a sprite or background.
